Statistics for People is a blog with the purpose to break down the complexity of statistics and to rather focus more on its implications for society, politics, art and many more. This blog aims to address all kinds of people who are interested to understand to what extent Statistics, Machine Learning and Artifical Intelligence have an impact on our daily lives. Some posts will address more advanced readers and others will be understandable without any preliminaries, execpt for the interest our even joy of statistics.

## Notable Features

- Bundled as a "theme gem" for easier install/upgrading.
- Compatible with GitHub Pages.
- Support for Jekyll's built-in Sass/SCSS preprocessor.
- Nine different skins (color variations).
- Several responsive layout options (single, archive index, search, splash, and paginated home page).
- Optimized for search engines with support for [Twitter Cards](https://dev.twitter.com/cards/overview) and [Open Graph](http://ogp.me/) data
